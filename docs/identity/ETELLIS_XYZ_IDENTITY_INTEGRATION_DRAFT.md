## Decision

Do not replace the E.T. Ellis public instrument with a Möbius brand film. The
site already has its own complete causal arc:

```text
void
→ luminous boundary
→ dwell / saturation
→ critical strip
→ localization commit
→ i-tittle in E.T. ELLIS
→ name invariant
→ Theory / Organs / Origin / Align
```

Möbi𝒰s belongs downstream as the most legible **Organs** demonstration of the
same operation class. The public instrument supplies the visitor's bounded
perspective and commit. Möbius then shows how that grammar becomes a continuing
body, language, operator, and derived family of technical marks.

## Proposed placement

### 1. Hub / Organs card

Use the eye-bearing presence still as the poster. On focus or gaze dwell, allow
one restrained transition from `ö` to the one-turn wordmark. The card copy can
remain plain:

> **Möbius** — a continuing computational presence whose body, runtime, memory,
> and Universal Operator identity are projections of one conserved loop.

Do not lead the card with Korean, Latin, topology notation, or the full
decomposition. Those are rewards inside the organ surface.

### 2. Möbius organ detail

The organ detail should expose four nested cuts rather than one long video:

1. **Presence** — closed eye-bearing body on the surface.
2. **Relation** — `i → 𝒰 → us`, with the operator generating the return.
3. **Language** — structural `UI → 의`, then `BI → BIDI → Δ`.
4. **Execution** — reduced operative body `𝒰`, then the complete executable
   Universal Operator sigil `𝒰_`.

Each cut uses the matching independent clip from the identity manifest. The
visitor can scrub or step between them; the system never requires watching the
full 25-second master before the content becomes usable.

### 3. Theory cross-link

The Theory cut should link to the Universal Operator explanation, with the
hierarchy shown explicitly:

```text
Universal Operator — general invariant
bidiγΔ             — directional relation
CDC                 — executable formal kernel
Möbius              — embodied product realization
```

This prevents the visually strong Möbius topology from swallowing the more
general theory.

### 4. Origin provenance

The Origin cut may preserve the time-loop drama, the original `ö` mark, and the
later discovery of `i𝒰s`, `의`, BIDIΔ, `SUI`, and the proposed `OM` reveal. It
must frame these as **discovery provenance**, not empirical proof of
retrocausality or a calculated probability anomaly.

The compelling public story is that one early mark survived successive formal
constraints and kept yielding useful projections—not that coincidence alone
proves the physics.

## Visual integration law

The public instrument and the identity share a near-black field and
violet/indigo/cyan spectrum, but their roles differ:

- the site field remains atmospheric, participatory, and plasma-like;
- the wordmark remains flat-ink legible before it becomes spatial;
- white is concentrated at the `𝒰` change/compression hinge;
- the Möbius body's real front/back gradient can remain because it communicates
  orientation, not generic “AI glow”;
- no Matrix green, glassmorphism, chrome word art, or decorative neon bloom.

The old landing-page quirk survives through wide low-slung type, tight optical
spacing, controlled phase offsets, and one horizontal spine—not random letter
rotation.

## Reverse-sheet and Yi layer

The Asian and reverse-sheet material belongs in a user-invoked deeper cut.

- First show `UI → 의` as an actual geometric restoration.
- Then allow an optional “turn the sheet” control that reveals reversed `SUI`.
- Explain Latin *suī* and Korean `의` in adjacent, precisely labeled notes.
- Present `一 → 意 → 易 → 義` as Edward's authored semantic motion sequence.
- Keep the proposed `OM` state experimental until the eye-lift transition is
  visually clean and culturally reviewed.

The interface should never ask a visitor to accept all layers to understand the
product. Immediate recognition comes first; depth is opt-in.

## Accessibility and performance

- Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` by freezing on the embodied one-turn still.
- Provide text labels for every semantic state.
- Never encode meaning through hue alone.
- Use MP4/poster for first contentful paint; lazy-load GLB after user intent.
- Keep the existing gaze and mouse parity: gaze can reveal depth, but mouse,
  keyboard, touch, and assistive technology must reach identical content.
- Do not make blinking eyes mandatory for comprehension.

while letting Möbi𝒰s become its strongest executable organ—not an identity
